Indian Arts

Indian art includes classical dance, music, painting, sculpture...

Bharatanatyam

Originating from Tamil Nadu, Bharatanatyam is known for its grace, purity, and intricate movements. It is one of the oldest classical dance forms in India, characterized by its fixed upper torso, bent legs, and intricate footwork.

Kathakali

Hailing from Kerala, Kathakali is a classical dance-drama known for its elaborate costumes, makeup, and expressive gestures. It typically depicts stories from Hindu epics like the Mahabharata and Ramayana.

Madhubani Painting

Also known as Mithila painting, this folk art form from Bihar is characterized by vibrant colors, intricate patterns, and themes related to nature and mythology.

Warli Painting

Warli painting is a traditional tribal art form from Maharashtra, created by the Warli tribe. The paintings are known for their simple yet dynamic representations of daily life and nature, using geometric shapes like circles, triangles, and squares.
